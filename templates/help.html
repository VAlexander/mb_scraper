Need some help?

To start scraping, press the "Start scraping" button located in the main page.

You'll be redirected to the tool's page. Here you need to fill all the fields.

"Artist" field should contain artist name or its part like it is written on mediabase.com (but case is not important). For example, "BIEBER, JUSTIN", "BIEBER", "Bieber" are equal in this case.

"Song" field should contain song name or its part like it is written on mediabase.com

"Threshold" field should contain a number of the position to look for. For example, "5" will tell the scraper to compare results with top-5

"No. of days" should contain the number of days of the report you want to get.

At last, check all the needed checkboxes on the right and press "Scrape" button.

Scraper needs some time to process requests, so please be patient.

When it's done, you'll get the results on the screen.

To email the results simply enter your email address into the "email" field and press "Mail this" button.
